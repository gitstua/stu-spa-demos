How to Play Dare Ya Cobber

Game Rules:

  1. Choose a dare card to challenge your mate
  2. Click "Dare a mate" to get a unique challenge URL
  3. Share the URL with your friend
  4. Your friend completes the dare and can dare you back
  5. Used cards are greyed out and can't be reused
  6. Keep the fun going by daring each other back and forth!

Tips:

  • Cards are organized by themes like Fun, Food, and Challenges
  • All dares should be fun and safe - no harmful challenges!
