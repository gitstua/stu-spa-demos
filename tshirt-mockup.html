T-Shirt Text Mockup Studio

Mock up bold, simple t-shirt concepts without leaving the browser. Tune the shirt color, typography, sizing, and placement, then share the URL to keep the mock in sync.

How it works: Every control updates a compact set of settings stored in the URL fragment (everything after the #). NRL team presets are just shorthand for shirt and text color pairs, and the full state is still encoded in the URL hash. When you copy the shareable link, anyone opening it sees the exact same mockup without any saved data or accounts.
